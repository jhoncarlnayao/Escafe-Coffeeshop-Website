What we are
actually building.

More than just a café, Escafe is built around experiences people remember — meaningful coffee, genuine connections, and a space that quietly feels like an escape from everyday life.

01

Origin excellence. Carefully selected beans crafted to bring warmth, depth, and consistency in every cup.

02

Our story

Three years in.
Still pouring.

The menu changed. The music changed. What we are trying to do has not.

2021
First location

Maria found an old bakery on a quiet street in Maginhawa. Three months of renovation. Soft-opened to 40 people who told 400.

2022
Roastery opened

Started roasting on-site. Andres joined. For the first time we controlled the full process from green bean to cup.

2024
Second location

Opened our second branch in BGC. Same obsession, different neighborhood. Eight months to hire six people. Intentional.

2026
Now

840 daily visitors. Two roasters. Twelve farm partnerships. Series A conversations have begun — on our terms.

2027
What is next

Third location. Subscription roastery. Training academy. And a real café garden we have been dreaming about since day one.
